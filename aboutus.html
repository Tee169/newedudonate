ABOUT EDU-DONATE

Edu-Donate is a foundation that is dedicated to ensuring quality education in communities in line with

SDG goal 4 "Quality Education". We work with diiferent commuinties

But at the moment our current donations is for the commuinty of Achara, located at Ikwuano local Govt. Area of Abis state

We encourage you to donate with us to enable us fund kids in the commuiunity in tech and other educational activities.

With your money we will be able to train children in this community in tech, sports, musical instruments and other activities that will help the commuinty grow.

Children in undeserved communities should not be left behind, they should also get an opportunity to basic learning and Education.

Every donation you make to is helping a child in this community learn how to use a computer and do basic work with it learn a music instrument "Music keeps the soul happy" and also engage in other activities.





OUR GOALS
  • TO MAKE SURE CHILDREN IN UNDESERVED COMMIUNTIES HAVE ACCESS TO EDUCATION
  • QUALITY EDUCATION
  • PROVIDE QUALIFIED TEACHERS/TRAINEES FOR THE CHILDREN
  • COMMUNITY GROWTH AND DEVELOPMENT THROUGH QUALITY EDUCATION
  • INTRODUCE THE CHILDREN INTO THE TECH WORLD
