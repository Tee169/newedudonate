ABOUT ACHARA COMMUNITY

The community of Achara in Orsu local govt area of imo state is a calm,

wonderful community, with great indigenes.

They practice farming and handcrafts and also survive on marketing their farm produce

They also have very culture lots of cultural celebration ranging from

different masqurade dance to new yam festival to mention just a few.

And just like every other child out there we believe the children of Achara coomunity

deserve to have basic education like other normal children and thats

why we decided to step in.

EDUCATION

The community of Achara does not have basic amenities and standard facilities for basic Education

with just one existing school building, few chairs and very few teachers to train the kids

it it not a suitable suituation for the children growing here

And this has left hundreds of the children out of school and rather farming with their parents.

With that the children in this community lack basic quality education

But with your donation these children can learn and acquire great knowledge.

And that is why we believe this initiative is necessary and beneficial to the children in this community.
